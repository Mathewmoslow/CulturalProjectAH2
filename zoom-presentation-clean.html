Health Disparities & Diabetes Prevalence

03 / 23
Epidemiology

Native American communities experience Type 2 diabetes at nearly double the rate of the general U.S. population. This disparity reflects complex interactions between historical trauma, socioeconomic barriers, and cultural factors affecting health outcomes.

Key Statistics

13.6% prevalence among Native Americans vs. 7% in general population
